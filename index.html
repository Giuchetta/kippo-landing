Questions Parents Ask

Short answers on how Kippo works, what families get, and how we handle privacy.

What is Kippo?

Kippo creates personalized audio stories starring your child. Each story is made for listening, with warm narration, original music, and details pulled from your child’s world.

How does personalization work?

You share a few simple details like your child’s name, age, interests, and personality. Kippo uses that to shape stories that feel familiar, encouraging, and made just for them.

How does voice cloning work?

On eligible plans, you can record your own voice so stories sound even more personal. It’s optional, parent-controlled, and only available on plans that include cloned voices.

How many stories come with each plan?

Free includes 1 story each month. Storyteller includes 3, Storyteller+ includes 8 and 1 cloned voice, and Family includes 20 stories plus 5 cloned voices.

What if I run out of stories?

No problem — you can buy extra stories anytime for $2.99 each. They're added to your account instantly, never expire, and are only used after your monthly stories are gone.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. You can cancel whenever you want, and there's no long-term commitment. Your existing stories stay in your library and are accessible even after a paid plan ends.

How does Kippo protect kids’ privacy?

Kippo is built for families with child privacy in mind. We keep data handling tight, avoid ads and third-party tracking, and design the product to support COPPA-conscious use.
